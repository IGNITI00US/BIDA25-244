War Timeline

1914 – Outbreak of World War I

World War I begins after rising tensions in Europe following the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand.

1915 – Trench Warfare Dominates

Soldiers become locked in trench systems across Europe, leading to long and brutal battles.

1918 – End of World War I

The war ends with the Armistice signed on November 11, 1918.

1939 – World War II Begins

Germany invades Poland, triggering the start of WWII.

1944 – D-Day Invasion

Allied forces land in Normandy, marking a major turning point in the war.

1945 – End of World War II

Germany surrenders in May, followed by Japan in September after atomic bombings.

1989 – Fall of the Berlin Wall

The Berlin Wall falls, symbolizing the end of Cold War divisions.
